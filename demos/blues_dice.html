Blues Grammar keeps the 12-bar structure but substitutes chords by dice. Free Chromatic mode rolls just 3 chords — one each for the tonic, subdominant, and dominant structural positions — then locks them into the 1-1-1-1-2-2-1-1-3-2-1-1 pattern. The shape of blues survives; the harmony may not.
