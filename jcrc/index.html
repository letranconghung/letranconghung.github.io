about us

A group of inspirational and dedicated leaders in Anglo-Chinese School (Independent) Boarding School, we organise various events throughout the year to entertain the boarders here. Pledging 'to do all things necessary to uphold the good name and prestige of the boarding school', we aspire to make ACS(I) Boarding School the most fun and enjoyable in Singapore.
